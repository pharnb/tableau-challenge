The most popular stations are downtown.
The majority of riders are male or 30 years old.
There was a steady increase in riders between May and September, possibly due to a relaxation of COVID restrictions.
There is a sharp rise in riders between 5 and 8 am as people wake up and go to work, and then a second at 5 pm as people go home, into a sharp decline as people arrive home and stop requiring transport.
The average trip duration peaks in the teenage years and decreases as riders get older.
